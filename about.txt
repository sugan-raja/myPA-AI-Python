myPA-AI-Python
====================

About:
------
myPA is a free and open-source personal AI assistant designed to help users with daily tasks, answer questions, and provide conversational support using local LLMs (Ollama) and custom Python handlers.

Intent of Owner:
----------------
- To provide a privacy-friendly, extensible, and fully local AI assistant for everyone.
- To encourage learning, experimentation, and community-driven improvements.
- To make AI accessible and useful for personal productivity, learning, and fun.

Authors & Contributors:
-----------------------
- Project Owners: Sugan Raja and Gopikrishnan
- Original inspiration: Praveen Kumar Srivastava (see Credits.md)
- Contributors: Add your name here by submitting a pull request!

Micro Details for New Users:
----------------------------
- All AI responses are generated locally using Ollama (no cloud required).
- Voice and text input/output are supported.
- Handlers for jokes, reminders, weather, system info, and more are included.
- You can add your own skills by creating new handler modules.
- See README.md for setup and usage instructions.
- For help or to contribute, see CONTRIBUTING.md.
